About Pandora Grow Box

Pandora grow box was born as an idea to grow your plants in a fully controlled way. It is made with a Web of Things technologies, which means that you can take a look and control the box from anywhere that has access to the Internet.

LIGHT

Programable Full spectre LED.

SENSORS

Pandora grow box has many sensors inside and outside that allow you to monitor all the variables around your plants
